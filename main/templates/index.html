What is Django Hold It?

Django Hold It is designed to make serving dummy images for front-end application developments fast and easy. It simply takes kicking your image serving server on your local machine, hooked to a free port, and linking to them anytime with varied combinations, just to light up your development, and give a visual cue with custom text, as to what belongs where and what each image represent.
